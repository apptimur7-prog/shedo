SheDo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SheDo ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the SheDo Android app. SheDo is a school-time and focus management app designed to help parents or guardians manage lessons, allowed apps, and device protection on a child's device.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect or process the following categories of data:

  • Account information, such as email address, Firebase user ID, and sign-in provider, if you choose to sign in.
  • Schedule and lesson data, such as lesson names, start times, end times, active school days, and setup preferences.
  • Subscription and transaction data, such as subscription product ID, purchase token, order ID, plan type, and entitlement status.
  • Usage and analytics data, such as app events, onboarding progress, paywall views, purchase events, and protection status events.
  • Technical and device-related data that may be processed by integrated SDKs when enabled, such as app instance identifiers, diagnostics, and attribution-related signals.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app functionality, including schedule enforcement, app blocking, and parent-controlled settings.
  • To save or restore schedules across devices when a user signs in.
  • To process subscriptions and trial eligibility through Google Play Billing.
  • To understand app performance, onboarding completion, and subscription activity.
  • To improve reliability, security, and product experience.

3. Services and SDKs We Use

SheDo may use third-party services and SDKs, including:

  • Google Firebase, including Authentication, Firestore, and Analytics.
  • Google Play Billing for subscriptions and free trials.
  • AppsFlyer for attribution and event analytics, when configured.
  • Meta / Facebook App Events, when configured.
  • Superwall for paywall presentation and subscription flow support, when configured.

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies when their services are enabled.

4. Data Storage and Sync

Some information is stored locally on the device, including protection settings, local schedule data, and parent control state. If you sign in, selected account and schedule information may also be synchronized to cloud services that support your SheDo account.

5. Children and Family Use

SheDo is intended to be configured by a parent or guardian. If the app is made available to children or used on a child's device, we expect the parent or guardian to supervise setup, permissions, purchases, and account choices.

We use reasonable measures to protect data. Data transmitted off-device is intended to be sent using encrypted transport such as HTTPS/TLS where supported by the service provider.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page.
